Battery

Maintenance Tips

Maintenance Guidelines
Keep It Clean
Maintain Cleanliness

Keep the battery top clean and dry at all times. Use a moist cotton cloth for cleaning, avoiding woolen, silk, or synthetic materials that may cause sparks.

Prevent Corrosion
Apply Petroleum Jelly

Apply Petroleum Jelly (Vaseline) to the terminals and cable clamps to prevent corrosion. Avoid using grease, as it can lead to power loss and weaken the battery.

Maintain Levels
Regular Checkups

Ensure electrolyte levels are maintained and topped up with distilled water. Regular checkups help in extending the life of your battery.

Optimize Charging
Efficient Charging

Optimize charging practices to prevent overcharging and undercharging. Follow manufacturer guidelines for best results.

Safe Disposal
Eco-Friendly Practices

Dispose of old batteries responsibly through authorized recycling centers. This helps protect the environment and ensures compliance with regulations.
